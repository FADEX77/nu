About Lead City University

Lead City University (LCU) is a private university located in Ibadan, Oyo State, Nigeria. Established in 2005, it was one of the first private universities to be licensed by the Federal Government of Nigeria. The university was founded by the late Prof. Gabriel Ogunmola, a renowned biochemist and former President of the Nigerian Academy of Science.

Lead City University is committed to providing quality education that prepares students for leadership roles in various sectors of the economy. The university prides itself on its practical approach to learning, modern facilities, and experienced faculty members.

The institution has grown significantly since its inception and now offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. Lead City University is known for its student-centered approach to education and its emphasis on entrepreneurship and practical skills development.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (160 and above)
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Direct Entry candidates must have relevant A'Level or equivalent qualifications

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Relevant professional qualifications (where applicable)
  • Entrance examination and/or interview

Application Process

  3. Pay application fee
