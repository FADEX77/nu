About Afe Babalola University

Afe Babalola University, Ado-Ekiti (ABUAD) is a private university founded by the renowned lawyer and philanthropist, Aare Afe Babalola. Established in 2009, the university has quickly risen to become one of Nigeria's premier private institutions, known for its world-class facilities and quality education.

The university was established with the vision to reform education in Nigeria and produce graduates who are not only academically sound but also morally upright and entrepreneurially skilled. ABUAD is committed to providing education that meets global standards while addressing local needs.

The institution boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including one of the best university teaching hospitals in West Africa, modern laboratories, and well-equipped lecture halls. ABUAD's commitment to excellence has earned it numerous accolades and recognition both nationally and internationally.

Campus Facilities

Academic Facilities

Ultra-modern lecture theaters, e-library with over 100,000 e-books, well-equipped laboratories, and moot court for law students.

ABUAD Teaching Hospital

400-bed multi-system hospital with state-of-the-art medical equipment and facilities comparable to the best in the world.

Accommodation

Modern hostels with 24/7 electricity, water supply, and internet access. Separate hostels for male and female students.

Sports Facilities

Olympic-size swimming pool, standard football field, basketball courts, tennis courts, and indoor sports complex.

Entrepreneurial Facilities

ABUAD farms, bakery, bottled water factory, and other enterprises that provide practical training for students.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (180 and above)
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Direct Entry candidates must have relevant A'Level or equivalent qualifications

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Relevant professional qualifications (where applicable)
  • Entrance examination and/or interview

Application Process

  1. Purchase and complete the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay application fee
  4. Attend screening exercise (if applicable)
  5. Check admission status
  6. Accept admission offer and pay acceptance fee
  7. Complete registration process
