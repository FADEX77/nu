About Ajayi Crowther University

Ajayi Crowther University is a private university owned by the Supra Diocesan Board (West) of the Church of Nigeria (Anglican Communion). Established in 2005, the university is named after Samuel Ajayi Crowther, the first African bishop of the Anglican Church and a pioneer of education in Nigeria.

The university is located in Oyo town, Oyo State, and operates from the site of the defunct St. Andrew's College, which was the first teacher training college in Nigeria. Ajayi Crowther University is committed to providing quality education within a Christian environment, preparing students for leadership roles in various sectors of society.

The institution combines academic excellence with moral and spiritual values, aiming to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable but also of good character. Ajayi Crowther University is known for its serene environment, conducive for learning, and its emphasis on discipline and character development.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (180 and above)
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Direct Entry candidates must have relevant A'Level or equivalent qualifications

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Relevant professional qualifications (where applicable)
  • Entrance examination and/or interview
