About Madonna University

Madonna University is Nigeria's first private Catholic university, established in 1999 by Rev. Fr. Prof. Emmanuel Edeh. The university has the distinction of being the first private university to be approved by the Federal Government of Nigeria.

The institution operates on three campuses located in Okija (Anambra State), Elele (Rivers State), and Akpugo (Enugu State). Madonna University is committed to providing quality education within a Catholic environment, preparing students for leadership roles in various sectors of society.

The university combines academic excellence with moral and spiritual values, aiming to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable but also of good character. Madonna University is known for its strict discipline, high academic standards, and modern facilities.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (180 and above)
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Direct Entry candidates must have relevant A'Level or equivalent qualifications

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Relevant professional qualifications (where applicable)
  • Entrance examination and/or interview

Application Process

  1. Purchase and complete the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay application fee
