About Pan-Atlantic University

Pan-Atlantic University (PAU) is a private university located in Lagos, Nigeria. It was formerly known as Pan-African University until 2013. The university is an educational initiative of the Pan-Atlantic University Foundation (PAUF), a non-profit foundation established by members of Opus Dei, a personal prelature of the Catholic Church.

The university is committed to providing high-quality education that combines academic excellence with professional ethics and personal integrity. PAU is known for its emphasis on character formation alongside intellectual development, preparing students to become competent professionals who can make positive contributions to society.

Pan-Atlantic University is home to several prestigious schools and institutes, including the Lagos Business School (LBS), which is consistently ranked among the top business schools in Africa. The university's approach to education is characterized by personalized attention, practical learning, and a strong emphasis on ethics and values.

Campus Facilities

Academic Facilities

State-of-the-art lecture halls, modern case study rooms, computer laboratories, and a comprehensive library with extensive digital resources.

Yemisi Shyllon Museum of Art

Nigeria's first university museum housing over 1,000 artworks and artifacts representing Nigeria's rich cultural heritage.

Accommodation

On-campus accommodation for students with 24/7 electricity, water supply, and security.

Sports Facilities

Football field, basketball court, tennis court, and indoor sports facilities.

Recreational Facilities

Student center, cafeteria, relaxation spots, and gardens across the campus.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (200 and above)
  • PAU Entrance Examination
  • Interview

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • GMAT/GRE scores (for MBA and some MSc programs)
  • Work experience (for Executive MBA)
  • Entrance examination and interview

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay application fee
  4. Take entrance examination
  5. Attend interview
  6. Check admission status
  7. Accept admission offer and pay acceptance fee
  8. Complete registration process
