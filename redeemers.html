About Redeemer's University

Redeemer's University is a private Christian university located in Ede, Osun State, Nigeria. Established in 2005 by the Redeemed Christian Church of God, the university is committed to raising a new generation of leaders through a holistic, Christ-centered education.

The university moved from its original location in Redemption City to its current campus in Ede in 2014. The institution has grown significantly over the years and now offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines.

Redeemer's University is known for its strong emphasis on character development alongside academic excellence. The university's mission is to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable in their fields but also possess strong moral values and leadership skills.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (180 and above)
  • Post-UTME screening
  • Direct Entry candidates must have relevant A'Level or equivalent qualifications

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Relevant professional qualifications (where applicable)
  • Entrance examination and/or interview

Application Process

  1. Purchase and complete the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay application fee
  4. Attend screening exercise (if applicable)
  5. Check admission status
  6. Accept admission offer and pay acceptance fee
  7. Complete registration process
