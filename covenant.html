About Covenant University

Covenant University is a leading private Christian university in Nigeria, founded by Bishop David Oyedepo of the Living Faith Church Worldwide. Established in 2002, the university has rapidly risen to become one of the most prestigious institutions in Africa.

The university operates on a unique philosophy of "Raising a New Generation of Leaders" through a holistic approach to education that emphasizes character, learning, and leadership development. Covenant University is committed to pioneering excellence in education and raising a new generation of leaders who will positively impact their environment.

The institution is known for its strict discipline, high academic standards, and modern facilities. Covenant University consistently ranks as one of the top universities in Nigeria and has gained international recognition for its research output and quality of graduates.

Campus Facilities

Academic Facilities

State-of-the-art lecture theaters, modern laboratories, Centre for Learning Resources (library) with extensive digital and physical collections.

Accommodation

Comfortable on-campus hostels for all students with 24/7 electricity, water supply, and internet access.

Sports Facilities

Olympic-size swimming pool, football field, basketball courts, tennis courts, and indoor sports complex.

Research Centers

African Development Studies Centre, Covenant University Centre for Research, Innovation and Discovery (CUCRID), and other specialized research centers.

Other Facilities

University Chapel, Health Centre, Cafeteria, Shopping Complex, and Entrepreneurship Development Centre.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Five O'Level credits including English Language and Mathematics
  • Acceptable UTME score (180 and above)
  • Covenant University Scholastic Aptitude Screening (CUSAS)
  • Direct Entry candidates must have relevant A'Level or equivalent qualifications

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree with at least Second Class Lower division
  • NYSC discharge or exemption certificate
  • Relevant professional qualifications (where applicable)
  • Entrance examination and/or interview

Application Process

  1. Purchase and complete the online application form
  2. Upload required documents
  3. Pay application fee
  4. Attend screening exercise
  5. Check admission status
  6. Accept admission offer and pay acceptance fee
  7. Complete registration process
